The mxBoard-Module based on eBord 1.1.b from maax-design.de, whitch is also based on XMB-Forum. This was transformed for nukestyle-systems (Postnuke) by Trollix into the XForum.

Base was mxBoard 1.1.b, XMB-Forum 1.6b 2 (Magic Latern) and XForum 1.8.

The mxBoard team particularly hereby dissociates itself from this base. Neither the maax-design-Team, the XMB team nor Trollix can be made responsibly or liably for any problems with the mxBoard module.

Special thanks go to tequila, from the maax-design-Team, for make available the mxBoard source code.

Special thanks to all our beta-testers. Only with their help we were able to find many small and nesty things in the mxBoard-Module.
